Our Sweet History

Since 2004, Scoops & Shakes has been crafting delicious, handmade ice cream that brings joy to every scoop. What began as a small family-run stand has grown into a local favorite, with unique flavors made from the finest ingredients. We’ve always been passionate about creating sweet memories, one scoop at a time, and we're proud to continue delivering the same quality and care with every batch.

Our Products

At Scoops & Shakes, we craft ice cream using only the freshest milk and cream sourced directly from local farms. From classic favorites to unique flavors, each batch is made with care and the finest ingredients to deliver a truly delicious experience in every scoop.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Scoops & Shakes , we use eco-friendly packaging and source our ingredients from local, sustainable farms. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact while delivering the finest ice cream.
